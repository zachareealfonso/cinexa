# CINEXA — Movies & Shows Explorer

## How to Run
1. Download and unzip the folder
2. Open `index.html` in your browser

- If you're on VS Code, use the **Live Server**.

---

## Features

- Hero Banner — big spotlight at the top that auto-rotates between Trending, Popular, and Top Rated titles. Click the tabs to switch between them.
- Browse Sections — jump to Trending, Popular, Top Rated, Upcoming, Now Playing & Airing and Favorites from the nav or sidebar
- Category Rows — rows on the home page organized by studio or genre (Marvel, DC, Disney, Pixar, Studio Ghibli, Action, Sci-Fi, Anime, and more). Use the arrow buttons to browse through each row.
- Movies & TV Shows — switch between movies and shows from the  sidebar
- Search — type anything to search across movies and shows in real time. Hit the X to clear.
- Genre Filter Bar — filter results by genre using the pill buttons below the search bar (click the search bar and type to show the genre's)
- Sort — sort results by Popularity, Newest, Oldest, or Highest Rated
- Hover Preview — hover over any card for a quick peek at the title info without opening the full detail
- Detail Modal — click any card for the full breakdown: backdrop, poster, tagline, rating, runtime, genres, overview, cast, budget/revenue (movies), seasons/episodes (shows), and a "More Like This" row
- Watch Trailer — watch the YouTube trailer by hitting the "watch trailer" and it will send you to a new tab(must be online to watch on yt)
- Favorites — save any title to your favorites list. It stays saved even after you close the browser.
- Share — share a title's TMDB link directly or copy it to clipboard
- Where to Watch — quick links to Netflix, Disney+, Prime Video, Max, Apple TV+, and Paramount+ (open the side bar)
- Load More — load additional pages of results at the bottom
- Back to Top — button appears when you scroll down, takes you right back up

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts
- / — Focus the search bar 
- Esc — Close any open modal or clear search

- TMDB (The Movie Database) — https://www.themoviedb.org

Free public API. The key is stored directly in `app.js` since this is a frontend-only project.
